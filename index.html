Fragmented History

The world is no longer alive.

```

What remains are fragments—of cities, of memories, of something that once thrived.


Long before the fall, civilizations reached heights beyond imagination.

They built monuments that touched the sky, and machines that defied nature itself.

But ambition turned into ruin.

```

No one remembers exactly when it ended.

Some say the world was consumed by its own creation.

Others believe something ancient awakened beneath the surface.


Now, only silence remains.


You are not a hero.

You are not chosen.


You are simply… still here.

And in a world of ruins,

survival is the only truth left.

The Biomes

The Plains

Endless greenery reclaimed by nature. Beneath the tall grass lie the bones of forgotten towns.

  • Hazard Level: Low
  • Hostiles: Lions, Green Crocodiles
  • Point of Interest: Abandoned Village

Savanna

Golden lands that never sleep. Life thrives here—but so do the hunters.

  • Hazard Level: Medium
  • Hostiles: Lions, Tigers, Hyenas
  • Special: Savannah Village

Deep Forrest

Where light struggles to survive. The deeper you go, the quieter it becomes… until you realize something is watching you.

  • Hazard Level: High
  • Hostiles: Bears, Leopards, Snakes, Radiation Creatures
  • Point of Interest: Witch Hut

Dessert

An endless sea of sand and silence. The sun burns above, while danger crawls beneath every step.

  • Hazard Level: Medium
  • Hostiles: Coyotes, Scorpions, Snakes, Lizards
  • Special: Oasis Remains

Badland

A fractured land of fire and stone. Every step echoes across the cliffs… and something answers back.

  • Hazard Level: High
  • Hostiles: Sandstone Golems, Bandits, Desert Predators
  • Point of Interest: Bandit Outpost

Magma Mountain

Where the earth bleeds. The air itself is a weapon here, thick with ash and searing heat.

  • Hazard Level: Extreme
  • Hostiles: Lava Golems, Slimes
  • Special: Ancient Phoenix Altar

Rustpoint

A city that refused to die… and never truly did. The machines still remember their purpose—and you are not part of it.

  • Hazard Level: Very High
  • Hostiles: Drones, Turrets, Robot Units
  • Point of Interest: Central Facility

Blackrot Wasteland

Where life ends—and something else begins. The sky screams in purple storms, and the dead refuse to stay buried.

  • Hazard Level: Extreme
  • Hostiles: Zombie Beasts, Rotfang Behemoth, Plague Stalker
  • Special: Corrupted Ruins

The Guardians

Crimson Colossus

Born from a land that never healed. A towering force of stone and fire, awakened by the scars of the earth itself.

  • Type: Elemental Titan
  • Skill: Ground Slam, Lava Burst, Fire Aura

Infernal Titan

The heart of the mountain given form. Every step shakes the world, every breath burns what little life remains.

  • Type: Volcanic Titan
  • Skill: Magma Eruption, Lava Wave, Heat Surge

Sentinel Prime

A relic of the Great War. This AI war machine still follows orders from a dead commander.

  • Type: Mechanical Titan
  • Skill: Laser Barrage, Drone Swarm

Zombie Dragon

The ultimate corruption. A beast that refused to stay buried, now fueled by dark energy.

  • Type: Undead / Ancient
  • Skill: Corruption Storm, Poison Breath
